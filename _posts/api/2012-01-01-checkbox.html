This plugin add a checkbox to a node. You can parameter the initial state of the checkbox and disable it. there is also a 'checkbox.node' event added to the container. The checkbox hierarchy behaviour is as following:

  • Checking a child node checks all his parents, but doesn't affect his children
  • Unchecking a parent node, uncheck all his children, bu doesn't affect his parents
